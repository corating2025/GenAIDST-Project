Empowering English Language Education

A research-practice collaborative project aimed at empowering primary and secondary students in Hong Kong and overseas. We are committed to fostering students' creative autonomy, critical AI literacy, and English narrative literacy through creative storytelling.

Understanding AI and Large Language Models

Artificial Intelligence (AI) represents computer systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT are AI systems trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like language.

Digital Storytelling in Education

Digital storytelling combines traditional narrative techniques with digital media tools, enabling students to create compelling stories using multimedia elements like text, images, audio, and video.

The Intersection: AI-Powered Learning

When AI meets digital storytelling in education, it creates unprecedented opportunities for personalized learning, creative expression, and critical thinking development.

Creative Autonomy

Empowering students to make independent creative decisions and develop their unique storytelling voice.

Critical AI Literacy

Building understanding of AI capabilities, limitations, and ethical considerations in creative processes.

English Narrative Literacy

Enhancing English language skills through structured storytelling and narrative construction.

Collaborative Learning Framework

Our approach emphasizes collaboration between students, AI tools, and educators, creating a supportive learning ecosystem that promotes both individual growth and collective knowledge building.

Scaffolded Learning Process

  • Introduction to AI concepts and digital storytelling basics
  • Guided practice with AI-assisted story creation
  • Independent project development with peer feedback
  • Reflection and critical evaluation of AI collaboration
